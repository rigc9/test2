Participation Fee

Students: 75,000 yen
Others: 100,000 yen

Important Notice:

We have detected scam emails. Please be aware that official emails will include the organizer’s addresses: ryuhei.kohno.d4ac.jp & oyanagiac.jp.

The fee covers

  • - Hotel accommodation (4 nights, shared room for 3–5 people)
  • - Breakfast and dinner (buffet style)
  • - Lunch box (only on March 4)
  • - Banquet charge (without drinks)
  • - Coffee breaks
  • - Transportation (one way from Morioka Station)

Note:

For accounting purposes, the participation fee is fixed regardless of the length of your stay.
